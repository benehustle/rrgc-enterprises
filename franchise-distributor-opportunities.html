Frequently asked questions

Do I need an existing marine workshop to qualify in Australia?

Yes—partners must demonstrate undercover storage, hoists or gantries, and documented tool calibration. We reference Safe Work Australia expectations for workshop housekeeping during our audit visit.

How are NZ territories protected compared with Australian states?

We map exclusivity using regional council boundaries and population statistics from Stats NZ so Auckland partners do not overlap Waikato distributors. Agreements spell out ferry servicing rights where applicable.

Can I sell third-party accessories alongside RRGC craft?

Accessories must meet our electrical compatibility matrix. Anything that touches the CAN bus requires written approval so warranty coverage stays clear for your customers and our underwriters.
