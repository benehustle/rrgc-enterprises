Privacy policy for customers across Australia and New Zealand

Clear rules about the details you share when requesting quotes for autonomous electric craft and water cleaning systems.

What we collect

When you ring, email, or submit the contact form, we collect your name, phone number, email address, service interest, and the message text you choose to send. We do not run advertising trackers on this website and we do not sell contact lists to third parties.

How we use information

Details are used to prepare quotations, schedule site visits, and follow up on warranty or training questions. If you become a customer, we retain correspondence for seven years to meet Australian Taxation Office record keeping expectations unless a longer period is required for maritime compliance files you agree to in writing.

Storage and security

Enquiries sent by email reside within our Australian-hosted mailbox provider protected by multi-factor authentication. Paper job cards stored in Byford are locked outside business hours. If a data breach ever affects your personal information, we will contact affected individuals as soon as we become aware and outline remedial steps taken.

Questions about privacy?

Speak with our office manager for corrections, exports, or deletion requests—we respond within ten business days when verification is complete.
