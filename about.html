Our story across Australia and New Zealand

The same Byford crew answers the phone, assembles inflatables, and commissions cleaning hardware for councils, resorts, and families who want calm, electric time on the water.

Who we are

We are RRGC Enterprises, a family-owned Australian business with more than five years of hands-on work in autonomous vehicles. From our Byford base we sketch, source, and commission autonomous electric inflatable boats plus commercial water cleaning systems that suit everything from crowded metropolitan beaches to remote council-managed reservoirs.

We keep the conversation grounded: which battery sizes match your duty cycle, how you will launch without a heavy trailer, and what training your staff need before guests step aboard. Hospitality teams get polished deck plans, while government agencies receive maintenance schedules, spare parts lists, and documentation that lines up with local marine workplace expectations.

Our goal is simple—remove the noise of petrol logistics so families can enjoy the shoreline, and so authorities can keep waterways clear without burning through fuel budgets. When you ring us, you speak with the people who bolt the hardware together, not a scripted offshore desk.
